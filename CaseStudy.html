Barbaque Sweker

Barbecue skewers, also known as kabobs or kebabs, are a popular type of grilled food that consist of small pieces of meat, vegetables, and sometimes fruits that are threaded onto a skewer and grilled over an open flame. The skewer allows for even cooking and easy flipping of the food, while also providing an attractive presentation.

The 10 Best Kare-Kare

Kare Kare is a type of Filipino stew with a rich and thick peanut sauce.The traditional recipe is composed of ox tail or ox tripe. The vegetable components of the dish are string beans, eggplant, bok choy, and banana blossoms. Lightly browned toasted ground rice is used to thicken the sauce.
